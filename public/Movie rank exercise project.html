Top 5 Series Of All Times

1. Game of Thrones

Nine noble families wage war against
each other in order to gain control over
the mythical land of Westeros.
Meanwhile, a force is rising after
millenniums and threatens the existence of living men.

2. Breaking Bad

Walter White, a chemistry teacher, discovers
that he has cancer and decides to get into the
meth-making business to repay his medical
debts. His priorities begin to change when
he partners with Jesse.

3. Better Call Saul

Ex-con artist Jimmy McGill turns into a
small-time attorney and goes through a series of trials and tragedies, as he transforms into his alter ego Saul Goodman, a morally challenged criminal lawyer.
